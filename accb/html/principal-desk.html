Principal's Desk

“Education has for its object the formation of character.”

I feel privileged and honored to launch the website of PRB Arts & PGR Commerce College, Bardoli affiliated to Veer Narmad South Gujarat University, Surat (Gujarat).

Since the establishment of the college in 1963, the college has taken lead in catering contemporary and value-based education to students in this region, historically renowned for Bardoli Satyagraha under the leadership of Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, the iron man of India.

It has carved a niche in the educational climate of the university in particular and South Gujarat region in general.

I presume that the launch of the website will help us highlight the academic and socio- cultural happenings on the campus and put the college and Bardoli Pradesh Kelavani Mandal on global map.

I invite all the well-wishers, alumni and students to create an atmosphere of dialogue through the medium of this website.

Your suggestions, queries and comments will certainly buttress the academic climate on the campus.

I/C. Principal,
Dr. VIKRAM G. CHAUDHARI

M.A., UGC - NET, Ph.D.
