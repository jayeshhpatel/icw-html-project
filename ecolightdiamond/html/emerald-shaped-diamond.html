Emerald Shaped Diamond

An emerald cut diamond glitters with grace. The cheapest amongst all, this stone is prominently rectangular in shape but is available in square shape as well. Our certified emerald cut diamond is chiseled in step cuts and provides a large surface table with sides usually cropped. It creates a hall of mirror effect and emits both white and colored light.

Emerald Shaped Diamond

This cut was originally used for emeralds which was later adapted for diamonds thus the name derived as emerald cut diamond. The emerald shape diamond is a popular choice for those who wish to buy an elegant diamond at a lesser price.
