Our Businesses

In a world of averages, we are Specialists in manufacturing Round Belgium Cut Diamonds.
We deliver 99% accuracy and no variations. Precision and perfection are engrained in our culture.

Procuring

Quality raw materials yield quality products

We source quality raw material from across the globe.
This has enabled us to have an extensive inventory of finished products thereby ensuring a consistent supply of diamonds to all our customers. No matter how large an order is, we are always able to fulfill our clients’ requirements.

We source our rough diamonds from reliable sources located in Antwerp, Botswana, Namibia, South Africa, Canada and Israel.

Manufacturing

Delivering perfection consistently

Our manufacturing plants are based in Surat with distribution centers in Mumbai, Hong Konga, and Belgium.

A team of skilled artisans and hi-tech automated machines transform each rough stone into a majestic diamond.We measure and record everything. Our Maker & Checker policy is integrated in every step of diamond manufacturing. This ensures that errors are caught and fixed early on.

To significantly reduce human error, we have designed our ERP from ground-up. Our systems are error proof and extremely reliable. With a steady influx of raw stock, the strength of over 600 people, latest automated machinery, and global distribution centers, we are equipped to supply larges batches of precisely cut diamonds to our customers year after year.

Polishing

It takes skill to turn a rock into a brilliant diamond

We are known for our precision and quality. The Cut, Clarity and Colour batches of our diamonds are consistent with each order. There are no errors and no mix-ups.

Right from procuring raw stones to grading, our products go through skilled hands with an eye for detail. Every step in our process chain has our ‘Maker & Checker’ policy integrated in it. This ensures that our finished good are perfect.

Our inventory is extensive and this prevents us from contacting other vendors for goods, resulting in cost reductions and efficient operations.

Best known for our round Belgium cut diamonds, we delivery
consistency, precision and perfection in every batch, batch after batch.
